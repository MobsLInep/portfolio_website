The neon-cyan-on-dark palette is acknowledged to overlap with common "AI-generated" fingerprints.
Owner is **open to toning it down** — treat that overlap as a legitimate finding and recommend
where to dial back neon/glow toward something more distinctive and intentional, rather than
defending it as untouchable brand.
